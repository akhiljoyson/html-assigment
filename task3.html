About Us

The National Singing Competition 2026 is a platform created to discover and promote talented singers from across the country.

Our mission is to give young artists an opportunity to showcase their voice, gain recognition, and build confidence.

Every year, thousands of participants join to compete in a fair and inspiring musical environment.

Competition Rounds

Audition Round

Participants perform a song of their choice before judges.

Semi-Final

Selected singers compete live on stage.

Grand Finale

Finalists compete for the championship trophy.
